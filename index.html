The various physical activity groups can be classified as:
  • Sedentary
    Sitting most of the day with no structured exercise
  • Moderately active
    • Sedentary or low active job with 1 hr exercise daily
    • Active job (moderate movement 8+ hrs per day) but no structured exercise
  • Vigorously active
    • Active job (moderate movement 8+ hours per day) and 1 hr exercise per day
    • Sedentary or low active job but 2 hours of exercise daily
  • Extremely active
    • Training more than 2 hrs per day
    • Moderately active job (walking all day) plus at least 1 hr of exercise daily
Created according to the Mifflin St. Jeor equation

Tips on maintaining a Diet using the Calculator:
  • Maintaining: In-order to Maintain your weight it is recommended to follow the maintenance Calories
  • Bulking\Loading\Gaining: Inorder to put on weight, consume 250-500 calories more than your maintenance Calories
  • Cutting\Loosing: Inorder to loose weight, consume 250-500 calories less than your maintenance calories.
